About Us

At Perfect Tea, we draw our inspiration from our most recent journey to India. Our founders, Natalia and Danielle, embarked on this adventure, and their love for tea culture and exquisite flavors led them to establish Perfect Tea in the early months of 2023.

We hold a strong belief that tea should be savored in its purest form before considering any sweeteners or additives. With a commitment to delivering an authentic tea experience, every cup is freshly brewed to order. Our tea selection features an array of handpicked blends, each curated to captivate your senses and transport you to a world of delightful aromas and tastes.
